Humidity

This is analyzing if the latitude has any sort of relationship with the humidity. Although it is mostly scattered all around, generally there is a 60% of higher humidity level in most areas. The r-value for the northern hempishere is 0.2075, and 0.1745 for the southern hemisphere. The linear regression model is trying to explain if there is there is a linear relationship between latitude and humidity. With very low r-values, it can't be assumed that there is a relationship between humidity and latitude. According to the linear equation plotted, what could be concluded is that a higher latitudes tend to have higher humidity levels. But one can note that the humidity level will likely be at around 50%.
