Cloudiness

This is analyizing how cloudy it gets depending on the latitude. According to the plot, there isn't much of relationship between the latitude and cloudiness since regardless of latitude the cloudiness percentage is more in the extremes (0 and 100%) The r-value for the northern hemisphere is 0.0816, and 0.1791 for the southern hemisphere. The linear regression model is explaining that there isn't really a linear relationship between the latitude and cloudiness, especially with very low r-values. The value for the northern hemisphere is even lower and has more data. As for the southern hemisphere, if we had more data, we can assume that the r-value will also decrease. Most are clustering at the extremes of the cloudiness.
