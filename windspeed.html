Wind Speed

This analyzes whether the wind speed has any relationship with the latitude. According to the plot, the wind speed is pretty consistent regardless of latitutde, staying within the 0-10mph range. The r-value for the northern hemisphere is 0.0656, and -0.0148 for the southern hemisphere. There is almost no relationship between latitude and wind speed at all. This can be seen by the r-values and the slope of the linear equations. The slope is almost 0, and so it would assume that the wind speed is about 7mph throughout, which is impossible. They do have fairly close intercepts. Most data points are clustering around the wind speed of about less than 10 mph.
